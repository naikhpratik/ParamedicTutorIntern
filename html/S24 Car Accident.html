24. Car Accident

You are called emergent to a 20 year old male patient who has driven into a tree. Another motorist called 911 from their cell phone but they are gone. Patient is alert and oriented walking around talking on his cell phone when you get there. There is very minor damage to the car and no starring of the windshield. Patient states that he was texting and driving and his dad is going to be upset when he gets there. Patient has no complaints, no pain, remembers the entire event and doesn’t want to go to the hospital.
  • For this patient, it would be best to:

    1. Take spinal precautions and transport patient
    2. Tell patient he is unable to refuse and transport him to the hospital
    3. Have patient sign refusal form and leave
    4. Allow patient to drive away after giving his name

  • The correct answer is 3. Have patient sign refusal form and leave.

    This patient has no complaints and doesn’t want to go to the hospital. Because you have made contact with this patient, it would be best to have them sign a refusal form saying they don’t wish to go to the hospital.
